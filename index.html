Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

Motivatto collects minimal personal data. We may collect:

  • Email address (if You contact support)
  • Subscription status information (via Apple and RevenueCat)
  • User-selected preferences

We do not collect payment card details directly.

2. User Preferences

To generate personalized motivational content, the Application stores selected preferences such as:

  • Age range
  • Gender preference
  • Motivation type
  • Voice type
  • Character style
  • Notification schedule

Preferences are stored locally on Your Device.

3. AI Processing

Motivatto uses ElevenLabs to generate AI-powered motivational audio.

When generating content, selected preferences (voice type, motivation category, style) may be securely transmitted to ElevenLabs. No personally identifiable data (such as name or payment details) is transmitted.

By using AI features, You consent to this limited processing.

4. Usage Data

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Feature interaction data
  • Anonymous diagnostics

This data is used solely for service improvement and security.

5. Subscription & Payments

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple App Store. RevenueCat is used for subscription validation. We do not store payment information.
