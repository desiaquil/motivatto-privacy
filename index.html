Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Motivatto is designed to collect minimal personal data.

We may collect:

  • Email address (only if You contact us for support)

We do not require account registration to use core features unless necessary for subscription validation.

We do not collect names, postal addresses, or payment card details directly.

2. User Preferences (Core App Functionality)

To provide personalized motivational experiences, the Application collects preferences voluntarily selected by You, including:

Motivatto uses third-party AI service providers, including ElevenLabs, to generate personalized motivational audio.

We do not transmit personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or payment information to ElevenLabs.

All Service Providers are contractually required to apply privacy and data protection standards equal to those described in this Privacy Policy.

Your use of AI-generated features constitutes consent to this limited data processing.

You may withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the Application or resetting preferences within the Settings screen.

This may include:

Usage Data is used to:

We use analytics tools solely for internal improvement purposes.
