Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Motivatto ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Motivatto mobile application (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information. We may collect your email address only if you contact us for support.

User Preferences

To provide personalized motivation experiences, we collect preferences you voluntarily select within the app, including:

  • Age range
  • Gender preference
  • Motivation type
  • Voice preference
  • Character style
  • Notification settings

This information is used solely to customize your motivational content.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized technical and usage data such as device type, app version, feature usage, and interaction data to improve performance and user experience.

Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed securely through Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not collect, store, or process your payment information directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide personalized motivational videos and audio
  • Generate AI-powered motivational content
  • Maintain and improve app functionality
  • Process subscriptions
  • Respond to support requests
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party AI Processing

Motivatto uses third-party AI providers, including ElevenLabs, to generate personalized motivational audio content.

When you use AI-powered features, selected user preferences such as voice type, motivational style, category selection, and scheduling preferences may be securely transmitted to ElevenLabs for processing.

No personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or payment data is transmitted to ElevenLabs.

All third-party service providers are contractually required to provide data protection standards at least equal to those described in this Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics and Service Providers

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the app and improve functionality. These services may collect anonymized technical data.

We may share limited information with Service Providers strictly for operating, maintaining, and improving the Service. These providers are obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Storage

Your preferences are stored locally on your device.

Motivational video history is stored for up to 7 days and is automatically deleted thereafter.

We do not maintain centralized external databases of your personal preference data.

6. Legal Basis and User Consent

By using the Service and AI-powered features, you explicitly consent to the processing of your preference data as described in this Privacy Policy.

You may withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app, resetting your preferences within the Settings screen, or contacting us.
